Meet Hermes

Your personal AI assistant that actually gets things done.

Hermes lives on your computer, learns how you work, and takes care of the busy work — so you can focus on what matters.

What Hermes Does

Simple tools, real results

Find answers fast

Ask anything and get real answers. Hermes searches the web, reads documents, and brings back what you need.

Automate your routine

Set reminders, schedule check-ins, and let Hermes handle repetitive tasks in the background.

Chat from anywhere

Message Hermes from Telegram, Discord, WhatsApp, or Slack — wherever feels natural to you.

Gets smarter over time

Hermes remembers your preferences and learns from every conversation. The more you use it, the better it gets.

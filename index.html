Your personal AI assistant, one command away.

Hermes lives on your computer, connects to Telegram, and handles the busy work — research, coding, file management, web search — so you can focus on what matters.

Install Hermes

Three steps. Five minutes. You'll be chatting with your AI on Telegram.

1

Open your terminal

Press ⌘ + Space, type Terminal, hit Enter.

2

Paste this command

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/NousResearch/hermes-agent/main/scripts/install.sh | bash

Paste with ⌘+V (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+V (Linux), then press Enter.

3

Follow the setup

The installer runs for about 2 minutes. It will ask you to connect an AI provider and set up Telegram. Just follow the prompts — you'll be chatting with Hermes in no time.
